The Nine Types of Titans


Name
Picture
Brief Describtion
The Founding Titan
by screaming, can turn Subjects of Ymir into Titans, control the minds of Titans, and alter the memories and bodies of Subjects of Ymir, but only if the inheritor has royal blood or is in contact with a Titan of royal blood.It is connected to all Subjects of Ymir and Titans via paths.
Attack Titan
can receive memories from both past and future inheritors of the Titan. Guided by these memories, it always moves ahead, fighting for freedom.
Colossus Titan
also known as the god of destruction is enormous and can emit immense amounts of steam from its body like a blast furnace, at the gradual cost of its muscle tissue. The user is able to control the Titan's transformation power and blast radius.
Female Titan
can inherit abilities much more easily than any of the other Titans. It can also harden areas of its skin for extra offensive and defensive capabilities. It also has the ability to summon pure titans with its scream.
Armored Titan
is covered by hardened, armor-like skin, except in key areas necessary for movement, it also possess the ability to harden its arms and feet into sharper claws which can be used to climb structures such as the Walls.
Beast Titan
can take on the physical characteristics of different animals that vary between its inheritors, and it has devastating throwing capabilities along with the ability to harden its flesh.
Jaw Titan
is the swiftest of the Nine Titans and has sharp, hardened teeth and claws capable of breaking almost anything.
Cart Titan
is quadrupedal and has great endurance that allows it to stay transformed for long periods of time, as well as transform in rapid succession without needing to rest, hundreds of times per day even. Its speed rivals that of the Jaw Titan.
War Hammer Titan
can materialize various weapons and structures from its own hardened flesh. It can be controlled from afar through a cable of Titan flesh while the user remains hidden within a hardened crystal casing.
